Notification Settings

You can receive notifications about current or upcoming releases at a frequency of two or four weeks. These notifications are always sent on Sundays. It is also possible to be notified on the release date or the day a new release is announced.

All you need to do is configuring your preferred channels.




To activate telegram notifications do the following:

  1. Generate a registration ID below.
  2. Send this ID as text message to our bot metal_detector_notification_bot via your telegram app.
  3. Come back here again and reload the page.
  4. Finally you can configure which notifications you want to receive from the bot.
